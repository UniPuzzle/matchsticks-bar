Wine Glasses to a House

Two wine glasses are arranged from ten matchsticks as is shown.

2 Squares from 8

Remove 6 matchsticks so that only 2 perfect squares would remain.

2 Trapeziums to 3 Triangles

Move 2 matchsticks to form 3 triangles.

3 Squares from 5

Remove 3 matchsticks to get 3 identical squares out of the current 5 ones.

6 Squares to 4 (ID: 19)

Remove 2 matchsticks so that 4 squares would remain.

Reversing the House

Move one matchstick to make the house face east instead of west.
